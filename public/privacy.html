Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data security are extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Layboka AI collects, uses, stores and protects your information when using our AI-powered Shopify automation platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate, improve and secure our services.

  • Shopify store information including store URL, products and branding assets.
  • Customer interaction data such as chatbot conversations and support requests.
  • Billing and subscription details processed securely through payment providers.
  • Technical information including browser type, device data and analytics usage.

2. How We Use Your Information

Layboka AI uses collected information to provide and improve our automation platform.

  • Deliver AI chatbot conversations and product recommendations.
  • Recover abandoned carts and automate customer engagement.
  • Improve platform performance, analytics and AI response quality.
  • Provide customer support, billing management and account security.

3. Payment & Billing Security

All payments are securely processed through trusted third-party payment gateways including Stripe and Razorpay.

Layboka AI does not store full credit card information or sensitive banking credentials on our servers.

4. Third-Party Integrations

To provide automation and AI services, we may integrate with trusted third-party platforms including:

  • Shopify
  • OpenAI
  • WhatsApp APIs
  • Email delivery providers
  • Analytics and infrastructure services

These services operate under their own privacy and security policies.

5. Data Protection & Security

We use modern security practices and encrypted infrastructure to help protect customer information from unauthorized access, misuse or disclosure.

While no online platform can guarantee absolute security, we continuously monitor and improve our systems to maintain a secure environment.

6. Cookies & Analytics

Layboka AI may use cookies and analytics technologies to improve platform functionality, understand usage behavior and optimize user experience.

These technologies help us enhance performance, detect issues and improve AI interactions.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights related to accessing, updating or requesting deletion of your data.

To request account deletion or data removal, contact our support team through the contact page.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect platform improvements, legal requirements or security updates.

Continued use of Layboka AI after updates means you agree to the revised policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding privacy, billing or security, please contact us through the Contact page.
